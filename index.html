mChase

Multi-language text-to-SQL dataset

What is mChase?

mChase is a large-scale multi-language semantic parsing dataset for context-dependent Text-to-SQL task.

News

We have released the data of mChase of five languages(English, Chinese, France, Japanese, and Russia)
